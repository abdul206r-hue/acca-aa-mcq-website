ACCA AA MCQ Quiz

Question 1:

What is the purpose of an audit?

The purpose of an audit is to provide assurance that the financial statements are free from material misstatement.

Question 2:

Which framework guides the audit process?

The International Standards on Auditing (ISA) provides a framework for the audit process.

Question 3:

What is materiality in audit?

Materiality is the significance of financial information to users, guiding the audit's information and focus.

Question 4:

What is the role of internal controls?

Internal controls are processes designed to ensure accuracy and prevent fraud in financial reporting.

Question 5:

Why is independence important for auditors?

Independence is crucial for auditors to provide unbiased opinions and maintain credibility.
